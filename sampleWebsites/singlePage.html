About Us

Sally, Curator

Sally has traveled extensively to source timeless antiques, carefully curating each item to tell a story. Her eye for detail ensures that every treasure is authentic and meaningful.

Merril, Research & Catalog

Merril meticulously documents and researches each piece, providing historical context and insights. Her work allows visitors to truly appreciate the uniqueness of our collection.

At Timeless Treasures, we specialize in carefully curated antiques from around the world. Founded by two sisters with a shared passion for history and design, our journey began with a love of discovering unique, timeless pieces in far-flung markets, quaint shops, and hidden corners of every country we visited.

Over the years, we have traveled extensively, sourcing rare collectibles, vintage furniture, exquisite jewelry, and one-of-a-kind decorative items. Each treasure in our collection is thoughtfully chosen for its craftsmanship, story, and enduring beauty. Our mission is to bring these extraordinary finds to our local community and visiting enthusiasts, providing a space where history and artistry come to life.

Whether you are a long-time collector, a first-time buyer, or simply someone who appreciates the elegance of bygone eras, we invite you to explore Timeless Treasures. Our dedication to quality, authenticity, and personal service ensures that every visit is memorable and every piece you take home tells its own unique story.
